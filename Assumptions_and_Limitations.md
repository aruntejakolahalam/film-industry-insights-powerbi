# Assumptions and Limitations

- Analysis is based on the IMDb movie dataset available at the time of the study.
- Revenue figures are assumed to be reported consistently across films and years.
- Audience ratings are treated as a proxy for viewer engagement and satisfaction.
- The analysis focuses on historical data and does not include future forecasting.
- External factors such as marketing spend, regional releases, and streaming impact are not considered.
- Insights are limited by the completeness and accuracy of the source dataset.
